Fivio Foreign Talks Tory Lanez, Doubts If Megan Thee Stallion Was Shot

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Fivio Foreign‘s beliefs haven’t wavered about his stance on Tory Lanez and Megan Thee Stallion. The 33-year-old extended his support to the incarcerated Brampton artist and expressed his apprehension about the “Hiss” rapper getting shot.

“I f**k with Tory,” he said during his Monday (Feb. 5) guest appearance on Math Hoffa’s My Expert Opinion. “I know Tory as a real person. In my eyes, I don’t even know if Tory have any guns. I know him. I know his personality. I don’t see him as that type of person. I wasn’t there, I’m not a lawyer or no scientist but if somebody said they got shot and there’s no hole in their body then they didn’t get shot. We all know that but we know how this sh*t go.”

He clarified that he does not believe she is lying, but takes issue with the fact that if she did lie, she ruined Lanez’s life. “I’m not saying she lying but if it was a lie then she threw a man’s life away,” the Brooklyn drill rapper said. “That’s f**king crazy. I wasn’t there. I don’t know the logistics of it ’cause I don’t even look at the story that much.”

Fivio Foreign and Tory Lanez collaborated on “K Lo K” back in January 2020, and Fivio has been vocal about his support for the Chixtape artist.

As for Megan Thee Stallion, she’s been immersed in the latest hot rap beef over the last week or so. She had some words for Nicki Minaj, her husband Kenneth Petty, and Drake on her recent single “Hiss,” which went No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 this week.

Minaj fired back with her diss track “Big Foot,” which accused Meg of sleeping with her late mother’s former partner, having sex with multiple men in the industry, and much more. Listen to “Hiss” and “Big Foot” below.
