Here's How Idris Elba Earns And Spends His Millions

Idris Elba had generated a passion for acting and music from a young age and even attended music theater. He began his career in the 1990s in British television shows before moving base to the United States. Elba established himself as a prolific theater actor and landed a role of a lifetime in the drama series The Wire in 2002. His career has risen since then as he booked roles in notable projects such as The Office, The No. 1 Ladies Detective Agency, and Luther. For Luther, Elba also won a Golden Globe Award. Along with his flourishing acting portfolio, he has established a career as a producer and DJ, earning a fortune worth $60 million.

Idris Elba tries to maintain a low-key life with his family, but the actor does not hesitate to spend a few million on himself. He owns a stunning car collection and enjoys vacations with loved ones. Let’s look at how Idris Elba earns and spends his millions.

Earns: Acting Career

Idris Elba is one of the highest-paid actors in the world, as his movies have culminated in a box office total of over $10 billion throughout his career. After getting a breakthrough in The Wire, the actor earned less than $10,000 per episode for the HBO show. According to Parade, the actor was reportedly paid $10 million for his role in Sonic 2, $8 million to play Bloodsport in The Suicide Squad, and $8 million to star as the antagonist in Hobbs & Shaw. Along with being a DC actor, he was an integral part of the MCU, portraying the role of Heimdall in the Thor series.

Spends: Car Collection

Idris Alba has a lavish car collection that combines fast and affordable cars. Some of his most notable vehicles include Jaguar XE, Bentley Continental GT, Ford Fiesta F1 Rally, and a Toyota Avalon. As a racing enthusiast, he also has a Super Comp Dragster, which is only permissible to be driven on race tracks.

Earns: Production Company

The actor founded the production company Green Doors Pictures in 2013 to bring diverse stories to the big screen and uplift communities. This includes television shows, anime series, animated shows, and movies. Some notable projects include Turn Up Charlie, In The Long Run, and The Idris Takeover. His production company released Concert Cowboys in 2020 on Netflix with Elba in a supporting role. The same year, Green Doors signed a first-look deal with AppleTV+ to produce features and series for the streaming platform, as noted by Variety.

Spends: Special Celebrations

Idris Elba, a fan of parties and celebrations, does not hesitate to splash his fortune on lavish events for family and friends. As mentioned by Vogue, in April 2019, he married his longtime girlfriend, Sabrina, in a stunning ceremony in Marrakesh, Morocco, in front of 200 wedding guests. His wife was dressed in a custom Vera Wang wedding gown, while Idris opted for an Ozwald Boateng suit. He hosted wedding festivities for three days which also featured traditional Moroccan performances.

Earns: Business Venture

Along with running a production company, Idris Elba also has set foot into making profitable investments in various businesses. In December 2022, he invested an undisclosed amount in a plant-based food company Huel out of the $24 million raised in funding. Huel was launched in 2017 in the UK and provides snack bars, hot lunches, and ready-to-drink options. Over the last five years, the company has increased its valuation to $560 million. Elba will primarily work with Huel to contribute toward the climate change initiative.

Spends: Vacations

Idris Elba loves to wind down and relax when he is not working on projects. In 2019, he went on a honeymoon with his wife, Sabrina, to Tanzania and stayed at one of the best safari hotels in the world. Between his staying at his homes in the UK and America, Elba’s favorite vacation destination is Jamaica. The actor details that he loves the country's culture, people, food, and tropical vibes. He spent the first month of 2020 enjoying a break in a private villa in the Maldives.

Earns: DJ

Idris Elba’s uncle ran a DJ business, and the actor helped him perform at various events. In 1987, he started his DJ company and performed at events named Big Driis. While Elba shifted focus toward his film career, he returned to his passion in 2007. In the Summer of 2022, he took DJ Residency at Hï Ibiza, where he played every Friday night. Additionally, one of his biggest gigs occurred when he was asked to DJ Prince Harry and Meghan Markle’s wedding reception in May 2019, as stated by Elle.

Spends: Philanthropy

In April 2020, Idris Elba was elected as a Goodwill Ambassador for the International Fund for Agricultural Development. As the ambassador, he has been appointed to work in environmental conservation, climate change, and food security sectors. Additionally, he donated 100% of the royalties from his song One Fine Day to Black cultural organizations to bring change.

Idris Elba is a man of many talents and has also made a sizable fortune from endorsing various brands and starring in Super Bowl commercials. Over the years, he has cultivated his interest in DJing while continuing his acting career. The actor was last seen starring in the action horror Beast in 2022.
