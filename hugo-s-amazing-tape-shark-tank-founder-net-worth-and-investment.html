The Story Behind Hugo’s Amazing Tape

Hugo’s Amazing Tape was founded by Katherine Saltzberg and Lauri Fraser in honor of their father, Hugo Maisnik. Hugo, a multi-talented individual, was a magician, comedian, author, and inventor. He was known for his inventiveness and creativity, always seeking innovative solutions to everyday problems.

While performing as a magician, Hugo encountered the limitations of traditional adhesive tapes. These tapes often left a sticky residue on his props and lacked reusability, hindering the seamless execution of his tricks. Determined to find a better solution, Hugo set out to create a tape that would adhere to itself without leaving any residue behind.

Hugo’s vision became a reality with the invention of a revolutionary adhesive-free tape. This tape, now known as Hugo’s Amazing Tape, brought a fresh approach to binding and securing items. It adheres to itself through a unique static charge, making it reusable, durable, and residue-free.

The sisters, Katherine and Lauri, recognized the potential of their father’s invention and decided to pitch Hugo’s Amazing Tape on Shark Tank, the popular TV show for aspiring entrepreneurs. Their goal was to introduce the tape to a wide audience and share their father’s ingenious creation with the world.

Bringing Hugo’s Vision to the World

“We knew that Hugo’s Amazing Tape had the potential to revolutionize the way people use tapes. It was a tribute to our father’s ingenuity, and we were determined to honor his legacy by sharing his invention with others,” said Katherine Saltzberg.

The pitch on Shark Tank was met with enthusiasm and excitement. The Sharks were impressed by the tape’s unique properties and versatile applications, recognizing its potential as a game-changer in the industry. Ultimately, Mark Cuban and Lori Greiner made the entrepreneurs an offer they couldn’t refuse. They invested $100,000 in exchange for 100% equity in Hugo’s Amazing Tape, ensuring the product’s future success and availability to a broader market.

Since then, Hugo’s Amazing Tape has flourished, becoming a go-to solution for various binding and securing needs. Its adhesive-free nature, reusability, and strength have made it a favorite among crafters, DIY enthusiasts, and professionals alike. The tape’s versatility and durability have aligned with Hugo’s original vision, providing a reliable and convenient alternative to traditional adhesive tapes.

The legacy of Hugo Maisnik lives on through Hugo’s Amazing Tape, a testament to his inventive spirit and unwavering commitment to finding innovative solutions. The product continues to thrive, fulfilling its purpose and leaving a lasting impact on the world of adhesive tape.

The Shark Tank Pitch and Deal

In their Shark Tank pitch, Katherine Saltzberg and Lauri Fraser presented their innovative product, Hugo’s Amazing Tape, seeking a $50,000 investment in exchange for 50% equity in the company. The sisters were faced with a difficult decision but ultimately decided to accept the deal, entrusting the future of Hugo’s Amazing Tape to Mark and Lori. This investment deal marked a significant turning point for the company, opening up new opportunities for growth and expansion.
