David Schwimmer is an American actor and director with an estimated net worth of $80 million. Schwimmer's net worth and fame is predominantly for his role as Ross Gellar on popular NBC sitcom Friends. Friends aired on NBC from 1994 to 2004, co starring Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc and Matthew Perry. 

Schwimmer was born in New York city on November 2, 1966. He attended Northwestern University in Chicago and earned his bachelors degree in theatre. For the majority of the late 1980's he struggled with his acting career, and mainly had small roles in TV shows and made for TV movies. David's first appearance was in an ABC movie called "A Deadly Silence".

His career didn't get big until he played the role of Ross Geller on "Friends". In 2007 years after friends David directed his first movie ever entitled "Run Fatboy Run". Other than directing "Run Fatboy Run" after "Friends" he has mainly continued to act on stage in theatre productions. In 2010 he married Zoe Buckman. In May 2011 they gave birth to their first and only daughter named Cleo Buckman Schwimmer.
