Twiggy is an English model, actress and singer has an estimated net worth of $50 million.

Twiggy started as a model during the 1960s. Photographs of her haircut were displayed in the hair salon and a fashion journalist discovered her beauty.In 1966, she was named "The Face of 1966" by the Daily Express and voted British Woman of the Year. By 1967, she had modelled in France, Japan, and America, and landed on the covers of Vogue and The Tatler.

After she left the modelling industry, Twiggy enjoyed her career as a screen, stage and television actress. She has hosted her own series, Twiggy's People, in which she interviewed celebrities, and also appeared as a judge on the reality show America's Next Top Model. Her 1998 autobiography, Twiggy in Black and White, entered the bestseller lists. Since 2005, she has modelled for Marks and Spencer. In 2012, she worked alongside Marks & Spencer's designers to launch an exclusive clothing collection for the M&S Woman range.

Twiggy Lesley Lawson was born on September 19, 1949 in Neasden, London, England. She attended the Brondesbury and Kilburn High School in Salusbury Road, London. She married American actor Michael Witney in 1977. They had a daughter, Carly and their marriage ended with his untimely death in 1983. In 1984, she met Leigh Lawson and got married in 1988. Lawson adopted Twiggy's daughter, who took his surname.
