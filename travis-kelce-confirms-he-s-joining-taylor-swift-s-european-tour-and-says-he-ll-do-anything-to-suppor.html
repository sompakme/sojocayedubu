Travis Kelce Confirms He's Joining Taylor Swift's European Tour And Says He'll Do Anything To Suppor

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce will be joining Taylor Swift on her Eras tour in Europe to support her in her concerts.
  • Kelce expresses admiration for Swift's career and dedication, showing mutual support in their busy lives.
  • Travis Kelce's favorite tour stop so far has been in Singapore, but he wants to explore more.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been making the most of their downtime after a very busy year. Ever since their relationship started, they have been working non-stop in their intense careers, so they took a much-needed break to relax together. Now, recently returned from a romantic getaway to the Bahamas, they are getting ready to get back to work.

Taylor Swift is only about a month away from resuming her Eras tour, but since the couple doesn't want to be apart from each other, Travis Kelce confirmed that he will be traveling to Europe with her to support her every step of the way.

Travis Kelce Confirmed He Will Go On Tour With Taylor Swift

After the Super Bowl, Travis Kelce made it his mission to attend as many concerts of the last leg of Taylor Swift's Eras tour as he could before she took a break. Now, as she prepares to resume her tour, fans have been wondering if he would be in the audience this time as well. Travis didn't hesitate to confirm the news himself.

"Oh, you know I gotta go support," he said about it. He also expressed his appreciation for how supportive Taylor Swift had been, attending as many of his games as she could, adding that it's important to be involved in each other's careers, as they are both very busy.

"We are both very career-driven. I think we both love what we do and any chance that I can show my support to her — knowing that she has shown me all the support in the world throughout the season — it’s just been an amazing experience getting to know Tay," he gushed.

Travis Kelce also spoke about where he was most excited to see her play, with England taking the first place.

"I’ll tell you what, I think the London shows. I think she’s at Wembley [Stadium] eight times? Which is mind-blowing that she can do that many shows in one stadium and fill that thing up," he said. "And London is always an amazing city and Paris is a beautiful city and she will be all over Europe. There won’t be a bad show, I promise you that."

Travis' Favorite Eras Tour Shows So Far

Travis Kelce, of course, adores attending Taylor Swift shows, and he did so before they even started dating. Fans will recall that the NFL player attended an Eras tour concert where he planned to give Taylor his number, and left crestfallen because he didn't get to meet her at the time.

Of course, now that he's going as her partner, he gets to see another side of the shows and meet a lot of different crowds. In 2024, he attended concerts in Australia and Singapore, and he shared his honest opinion about which crowd he liked best.

Comparing the two places, he admitted that he hadn't spent enough time in Australia to get to meet the fans, so he chose Singapore as his favorite.

"I want to see more of Australia, I feel like I got to see a little bit more of Singapore. So I'm going to say Singapore was a little bit more interesting for sure," he said. "But I was only in Australia for a couple of hours," he quickly amended. "I want to see more of it, I'm intrigued, I'm there. The Aussies know how to have a good time man."
