Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills: Net Worth Ranking For Every Wife From Season 1 To Now

August 2024 · 6 minute read

While new faces on the Real Housewives can be exciting, we're all pretty attached to the housewives that we've been watching for years. RHOBH has been on the air since 2010 and with season 10 currently on the air, that's a lot of episodes to get obsessed with. While fans love all installments of the franchise, there is something particularly special about the fabulous ladies of Beverly Hills.

Some of the cast members are tough to deal with and others are lovely, which is something that we could probably guess from watching these ladies on TV. We're definitely wondering one big question, though: how rich are these women really? Keep reading to find out the net worths of these famous Beverly Hills residents.

18 Kyle's Sister Kim Richards Has $400,000

Kim Richards has a $400,000 net worth. We might wonder why that is pretty low, at least when compared to the other women who have appeared on the show.

But then again, Kim had her acting jobs when she was a kid, and she hasn't been in that many seasons of the show so she didn't make a high salary for years like some of the other ladies.

17 Taylor Armstrong's Got A Fairly Low Net Worth Of $500,000

Up next is Taylor Armstrong. According to The Cinemaholic, the former housewife's net worth is $500,000. We definitely feel for Taylor as she was in an abusive marriage during her time on the show.

16 Kathryn Edwards Has $2 Million Only

Kathryn Edwards wasn't on the series as long as some of the others but she does still have a pretty good net worth. According to Celebrity Net Worth, she has $2 million in the bank. We might remember that Kathryn's ex-husband is football star Marcus Allen.

15 Soap Opera Star Eileen Davidson Has $3 Million

Celebrity Net Worth puts Eileen's net worth at $3 million. She has been in tons of soap opera episodes and she's a working actress who also raises her family and is a great friend. We definitely loved seeing Eileen.

14 Brandi Glanville Is Back And Her Net Worth Is $5 Million

Celebrity Net Worth says that Brandi's net worth is $5 million. She's back on the show after being away for a while and that might make some of us happy, since she's always got something dramatic to say.

13 The Super Fabulous Erika Girardi Has $5 Million

Erika Girardi brings so much life, joy, and colorful clothing to the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. We never want her to leave as the show would feel her absence, that's for sure.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Erika's net worth is $5 million.

12 Lisa Rinna Is A Mover And A Shaker And She's Got $8 Million

We all recall when Lisa Rinna and Kim Richards fought and now we're wondering just how much money Lisa has. Well, Lisa Rinna's net worth is $8 million. That sounds like a good amount of money to us.

11 Garcelle Beauvais Is New To The Show And She Also Has $8 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, new face Garcelle Beauvais is tied with Lisa Rinna for a net worth of $8 million.

If we've tuned into the latest season, we're in the process of getting to know Garcelle.

10 Joyce Giraud De Ohoven Has $10 Million To Her Famous Name

Joyce wasn't on the show for very long but that doesn't mean that she doesn't have a fair amount of money in the bank.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Joyce has $10 million. Not bad. Joyce has worked as an actress and model besides starring on this reality show.

9 Up Next Is The Super Fit Teddi Mellencamp With $12 Million

What about Teddi? We all remember her place in Puppygate but let's finally move on from that and focus on her net worth.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Teddi has $12 million. She's super fit and has been running her own business for a while now. She's an accountability coach and we would totally hire her.

8 Famous Actress Denise Richards Has $12 Million

Denise Richards has a net worth of $12 million. That's totally what we were expecting since she is a very famous actress who has been working for a long time.

We love seeing Denise on the show and we definitely hope that she sticks around for a long time. We've seen too many familiar faces choose to quit lately.

7 Yolanda Hadid Was Beloved And She Has $45 Million

We all loved Yolanda, right?! Her lemon trees, generous heart, and her famous model daughters were all staples of RHOBH back in the day.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Yolanda has quite a high net worth: $45 million. This makes sense as she lives in fabulous places, takes amazing trips, and always dresses perfectly.

6 She's Been A Housewife And Friend, And Camille Grammer Has $50 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Camille Grammer has $50 million, which is very high. She was a member of the main cast at the beginning of the series, and we watched her get divorced from Kelsey and start to find her own footing. She has been back on the show recently in the "friend" role.

5 Adrienne Maloof Also Has $50 Million To Her Name

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Adrienne Maloof also has a net worth of $50 million.

Adrienne is one of the reality TV stars who got divorced after appearing on a popular show, as she and plastic surgeon Paul Nassif are no longer together.

4 It's $50 Million For Dorit Kemsley And Her Hubby, PK

Celebrity Net Worth says that Dorit and her husband PK have a "combined net worth" of $50 million. That puts her in the higher category of housewives, tying with Adrienne and Camille... which is interesting since Camille talked last season about how she didn't think Dorit really had any money.

3 Lisa Vanderpump Is Super Wealthy With A $90 Million Net Worth

We knew that Lisa Vanderpump would be on the higher end of this list but we might not have realized quite how wealthy she is. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Lisa has a $90 million net worth.

Whether she's wearing pink designer clothing or living in her mansion, Lisa does live a flashy but fabulous lifestyle.

2 Kyle Puts The 'Rich' In Richards With $100 Million

Kyle Richards has a beautiful family and her husband Mauricio's real estate business is totally booming and seems to get better every season. We were definitely expecting Kyle's net worth to be really high, and according to Celebrity Net Worth, she's got $100 million. Kyle was a childhood actress and has also recently gotten involved with some TV shows.

1 Carlton Gebbia Was An Actress And Racked Up $150 Million

Do we remember Carlton Gebbia from RHOBH? She has the highest net worth of anyone who has been on the show.

Carlton has $150 million, even beating out Lisa's $90 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
