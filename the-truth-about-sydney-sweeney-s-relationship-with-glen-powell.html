Every so often a celebrity couple comes along that virtually everyone seems to like. In some cases, that can be because they are adorable together on social media or during interviews and in others, it can be because they are popular individually.

Over the last several years, Sydney Sweeney has become one of the most talked about young stars in Hollywood. Famous due to the show Euphoria and other reasons, some people are so fascinated by Sweeney that they want to know everything about her fashion choices and how rich she is.

As of the time of this writing, Glen Powell isn't talked about quite as much as Sweeney is. That said, his career definitely seems to be on the rise due to his Top Gun: Maverick role and his undeniable charisma and speaking skills.

Given that Sweeney and Powell could quickly be in the same place in their careers and people are fascinated by both of them, there are lots of people who'd love to see them date. As a result, there was some excitement when there were headlines about Powell and Sweeney. That begs an obvious question, why is the press reporting on the two young actors together?

What Happened Between Sydney Sweeney And Glen Powell?

Back when the show Euphoria premiered on HBO, there were two main aspects of the show that people were talking about. First off, there was a lot of excitement that the always popular actor Zendaya was going to star in a new show. After that, people were fascinated by how extreme the events depicted in Euphoria can be.

Related: Zendaya Says 'It Just Felt Like The Right Thing' To Become A Producer On Euphoria

Not too long after Euphoria first became a sensation, however, a lot of people began to focus on another aspect of the show. Once viewers got to see more of Sydney Sweeney's Euphoria performance, she quickly became the show's breakout star.

Thanks to the notoriety Sweeney has gotten from Euphoria, she has gone on to land several notable roles.

On the television side of things, Sweeney landed a starring role in the first season of the immensely popular show The White Lotus. On top of that Sweeny has been cast in several movies. Most notably, Sweeney has been hired to play Julia Carpenter, a character who goes by Spider-Woman in the comics, in the upcoming Marvel movie Madame Web.

Long before Glen Powell starred in Top Gun: Maverick, he has been working consistently since the early-2000s. Some of Powell's past movie credits include roles in movies like The Dark Knight Rises, The Expendables 3, Ride Along 2, and Hidden Figures among others.

Powell has also notably become one of the actors the acclaimed director Richard Linklater loves to work with. After all, Powell has starred in Linklater's movies Fast Food Nation, Everybody Wants Some!!, and Apollo 10½: A Space Age Childhood. Linklater has also hired Powell to star in his upcoming film Hitman.

When looking at all the aforementioned projects that Sweeney and Powell have been a part of, it is clear the two actors haven't appeared onscreen together. However, as it turns out, that is going to end soon.

In March 2023, Sweeney and Powell were caught on camera looking pretty cozy together. Photographed seemingly vacationing on a boat together, a lot of people saw those images and assumed that Powell and Sweeney had become a couple.

While Sweeney and Powell clearly would make a gorgeous couple based on the photos of the duo wearing swimwear together, people's assumptions about them turned out to be wrong. In reality, Powell and Sweeney weren't vacationing together. Instead, the two actors were filming an upcoming rom-com they will headline together.

If anyone is disappointed that Sweeney and Powell aren't a couple, they may be focusing on the wrong part of this story. After all, rom-com fans should be excited they will get to see Powell and Sweeney portraying characters that fall in love on the big screen.

Who Is Sydney Sweeney Dating?

In the years since Sydney Sweeney rose to fame, her personal life has wound up in the spotlight a couple of times. Most notably, there was a lot of attention paid to Sweeney's family after photos of them wound up on social media.

Related: Sydney Sweeney Feels Like Her Family And Fans Don't "Understand" Her, Here's Why

Despite the harsh spotlight that Sweeney is under, however, most people have no idea about a major part of the actor's personal life. After all, many of Sweeney's fans are unaware that the actor has been off the market for quite a few years now.

In 2022, People published an article that revealed that Sweeney is engaged to a man named Jonathan Davino. Unlike Sweeney, Davino isn't in the public eye so their relationship has flown under the radar but the couple has reportedly been together for several years.

According to a Cosmopolitan article about Davino, he is a businessman who is 13 years older than his famous fiancée. In the past, Davino owned a pair of Chicago businesses and his family owns the companies 14th Round and Final Bell.

Is Glen Powell Single In Real Life?

Several years ago, Glen Powell's name began to show up in the tabloids. The reason for that is in 2016 and 2017, he dated Nina Dobrev and the press has always loved reporting on her dating life since she was involved with her former co-star Ian Somerhalder.

Related: Nina Dobrev: 10 Guys She's Been Romantically Linked To

While Powell and Dobrev's relationship didn't last too long, he has since gone on to date another woman for more than three years as of this writing.

Since 2020, Powell has been involved with a model named Gigi Paris. According to People, Paris has been a model since she was discovered at the age of 14.

Since her modeling career began, Paris has been hired by several high-profile companies including Mercedes-Benz, Revolve, and Nordstrom. On top of that, Paris appeared in the music video for Keith Urban's song "Somewhere In My Car".
