Dick Durbin’s Political Career and Achievements

Dick Durbin embarked on his political journey in 1983 when he successfully ran for the U.S. House of Representatives. Serving in the House for a remarkable 14 years, Durbin gained valuable experience and built a strong foundation for his subsequent political career.

In 1996, Durbin took his commitment to public service to the next level by winning a Senate seat. Since then, he has been re-elected multiple times and now stands as one of the most influential and respected members of the Congress.
