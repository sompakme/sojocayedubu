Irish singer Niall Horan has an estimated net worth of $10 million in 2013. He is best recognized as a member of the boy group One Direction. The band's other members include Louis Tomlinson, Zayn Malik, Liam Payne and Harry Styles.

Niall James Horan was born on September 13, 1993 in Mullingar, County Westmeath, Ireland. He was sixteen years old when he auditioned for The X Factor UK in Dublin on the show's seventh series. Along with four other boys who failed to move on in the competition, Niall Horan became a part of the popular group, One Direction, who finished third in the contest.

Eventually, One Direction was signed by Syco Music, a Sony Music subsidiary. Shortly after, the group's first studio album, titled Up All Night, was released. The album was a certified hit around the globe. In late 2012, the group's newest album, Take Me Home, was released.
