Who Is Elsa Hosk?

Elsa Hosk is a Swedish model and actress who came to limelight as Victoria’s Secret Angel in 2015.

Born on November 7, 1988, in Stockholm, Sweden, Hosk began her career as a model from a young age, with the IMG Models agency.

Hosk first walked on the runway for the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show in 2011 up until 2018. Since then, she has worked with numerous high-profile fashion brands and designers, including Dolce & Gabbana, H&M, Guess, Christian Dior, Dolce & Versace, Fendi, and Balmain.

Hosk has also appeared on the covers and pages of prestigious fashion magazines, including Vogue, Elle, Harper’s Bazaar, and Glamour.

In addition to her modeling work, Elsa Hosk has ventured into acting. She made appearances in films like “The Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show” (2015), the Belgian-French drama “The Unknown Girl” (2017), and the crime thriller “Galveston” (2018).

Hosk remains one of the prominent models in the fashion industry.
