Matt Lauer And Annette Roque Are On Better Terms Today

Once the allegations started coming in, it wasn't long after that Annette Roque and Matt Lauer ended their relationship. Things were taken care of on private terms, with Roque and Lauer agreeing on a massive settlement.

"The disgraced “Today” show host and his ex seemed to be on good terms as they reunited, greeting each other before Romy’s ceremony in East Hampton, New York, on Sunday."

The Finalized Divorce Settlement Saw Matt Lauer Fork Over $20 Million In Assets

Back in 2019, Page Six discussed the details of the finalized divorce. It should not come as a surprise, Lauer has forced in handing over several assets. It is said that Lauer wanted to ensure the best possible scenario for his ex. By the end of it, Roque walked away with over $20 million of their assets.

"We then revealed Lauer was “bending over backward” to give Roque — the mother of his three kids — everything she wanted in their divorce, according to friends. But the deal was not finalized till now."

"We’re told Roque gets up to $20 million in assets, and she’ll get to keep their Hamptons horse farm in Water Mill," Page Six stated.

Reports were conflicting however, as other platforms made mention that Lauer was not happy with the end settlement result.

There was another major question mark for the couple, and that was their massive estate in the Hamptons. With a worth of over $40 million, the lot was put on the market years ago and finally, it seems like there is some traction on the property.

Matt Lauer Finally Found A Buyer For His Hamptons Estate

According to Daily Mail, Matt Lauer finally sold the estate. In recent years, the former Today Show host brought down the price significantly, which once started at $65 million. Ultimately, according to Daily Mail, he got nowhere near that sum for the stunning estate.

"The luxe mansion, which features 12 bedrooms, two guest houses, a heated swimming pool and sweeping views of Peconic Bay, was bought by Lauer in 2016 from Pretty Woman actor Richard Gere in 2016 for $36.5 million - a steep discount from the $65 million the actor was originally looking for."

It remains to be seen what will take place with the funds of the residence, nonetheless, it is a some good news for Lauer, finally unloading the property. At the moment, the buyer is still unknown.
