2014: Bronfman launched HBFIT, a wellness and lifestyle website that offers tips and advice on fitness, nutrition, and mental health.

2015: She released a compilation album titled “Laser Certified,” featuring her remixes and original music.

2017: Bronfman published her first book, “Do What Feels Good: Recipes, Remedies, and Routines to Treat Your Body Right.”

2018: She married Brendan Fallis, a DJ and entrepreneur.

2019: Bronfman became the host of the podcast “HBFIT Talks,” where she interviews experts in wellness and fitness.
