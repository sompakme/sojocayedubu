When Is 'Vampire Academy' Season 2 Happening?

Mia's most recent performance of note has been as the main character Mia Karp on the show Vampire Academy. Its first season premiered in 2022 and many fans have wondered what the future holds for the show. Mia's character was in a relationship with another girl named Meredith on the show, and many fans have come attached to their romance.

Mia has talked about her character's romance with Meredith. She explained how important it was that the character's romance grew "organically". If and when the show continues, it's expected that their love continues to take center stage.

According to The Review Geek, 'Vampire Academy' has not yet been renewed for another season. That doesn't mean it won't be renewed anytime soon, especially regarding the fact the first season just ended. Only time will tell if the show goes on.
