About iJustine

American comedian, actress and YouTuber, Justine Ezarik, is most famous through her moniker “iJustine”. She first rose to fame with her iJustine.tv channel where she communicated directly with her millions of viewers from around the globe. She also launched her YouTube channel which, since 2006, has reached over a billion people.

As of 2023, iJustine has an estimated net worth of about $3 million dollars.

Justine Ezarik is from Santa Monica, California. She received the annual scholarship and studied at Pittsburgh Technical Institute graduating in 2004. In 2006 she became a finalist on the Yahoo! Talent Show, she also started her YouTube channel that same year. She actually joined the site before Google acquired the site in October that same year.

When Justine was just starting out she saw her online endeavors as more of a hobby that she hoped one day might pay the bills. Yet she was working on her career in graphic design and editing at that time. It would be more than a year before her career online would really start to take off.

In 2006-2007 making money online through YouTube didn’t come with a clear strategy. The idea of posting things about your life and what’s going on sounded ludicrous and to many the utmost vanity. Yet the truth is America loves to get the inside scoop and hear from real people. So when iJustine started to publish videos about her life, dreams, ambitions, tips and advice, it began to really build up traction.

“Vlogging” really wasn’t a thing yet, and in fact, many accredit iJustine with essentially creating it; or at very least popularizing the movement.

Through her two channels, iJustine and iJustine Gaming, Ezarik began building up a major following on the site and earn money through advertising. Aside from brief appearances on TV in “The Kill Point” and “MacBreak” Justine began focusing all of her efforts online. In fact between 2007 and 2009 she was lifecasting which means she was broadcasting essentially everything from her daily life online.

Nowadays Justine covers a wide variety of topics. You can find her videos about cooking, unboxing and testing consumer electronics, and her personal vlog posts. Her 300-page iPhone bill video was one of her biggest hits on the channel. As of 2023, her channel has 6.5 million subscribers many who actively devour her content.

iJustine has also now acted in several movies, including The House That Drips Blood on Alex, The Wedding Ringer, and appeared on shows like The Bold and the Beautiful, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Criminal Minds and The Vampire Diaries. She has also published the books I, Justine: An Analog Memoir and iJustine.
