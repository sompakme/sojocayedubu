Heartthrob Omari Ishmael Grandberry has an estimated net worth of $12 million. Omarion began his career after being discovered by a manager Christopher B. Stokes, for his vocal talent. His meeting Stokes led to the forming the group B2K. The band parted ways after a few years  Omarion began to work on his solo album. His first solo album was launched in 2005 titled O.

He has had a number of lucrative endorsement deals, such as "Reebok".

The young star has a degree in business and this has greatly helped him to manage his finances. The singer in addition to gaining from his music also plans to launch his own shoe line. The line is called "Oboi" which will feature collections that represent his personality and style for different occasions.

In 2010, Omarion joined as a judge on the fifth season of America's Best Dance Crew, replacing former judge Shane Sparks. He left after Season 5 to focus on his music career. He is set to launch his fourth studio album titled Love & Other Drugs in 2013.
