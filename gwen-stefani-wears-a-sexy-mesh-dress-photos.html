Gwen Stefani Wears a Sexy Mesh Dress: Photos

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Work it! Gwen Stefani wore a sexy mesh dress to accompany boyfriend Blake Shelton to his friend’s wedding in Nashville on Saturday, January 9.

The No Doubt frontwoman, 46, stunned in an avant-garde all-black mesh top, which she wore over a tight minidress.

To complete the stunning look, the “Used to Love You” songstress wore pointed stilettos, a black leather jacket and a shawl. Meanwhile, the country hunk, 39, looked dapper in a three-piece black suit and cowboy boots.

The hot couple has been in Nashville to attend the nuptials of Shelton’s friend and hairstylist, Amanda Craig. On Friday evening, the lovebirds were seen getting cozy and kissing on a dinner date at a branch of O’Charley’s restaurant.

“They were alone, pretty much all over each other,” an eyewitness told Us Weekly. “They were extremely affectionate, definitely not just cuddling. They sat on the same side of the booth, and no one bothered them at all. They looked very happy, all smiles.”

Us broke the news last November that the Voice coaches were dating after their splits from their respective spouses. Shelton divorced his wife of four years, Miranda Lambert, last July. Stefani and her husband of 13 years, Gavin Rossdale, announced their split in August.
