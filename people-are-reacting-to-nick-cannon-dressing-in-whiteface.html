People Are Reacting To Nick Cannon Dressing In Whiteface

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The innanets stay mad. We all know that Nick Cannon is prepping to release his next album White People Party Music on April 1. In addition to the standard single releases for promo rollout, he decided to fully embody the part… skin color and all. He spent the day as “Connor Smallnut” (lol), presumably his white alter ego. He debuted the spot on transformation on Instagram with this caption: “It’s official… I’m White!!! #WHITEPEOPLEPARTYMUSIC #Wppm in stores April 1st!!!!!!Dude Go Get It!!!Join The Party!!!! #GoodCredit #DogKissing #BeerPong #FarmersMarkets #FistPumping #CreamCheeseEating #RacialDraft “Bro I got drafted!!” Cannon spent time interviewing people under his porcelain identity and posted snippets of it on social media. Of course it got some of his followers in a tizzy. Some knew that it was meant in good humor, but others accused him of being a hypocrite (because Mariah Carey is his wife) and thinking “white face” is okay. He took to Twitter to chime in on the discussion, play into the outrage and ultimately tell people to chill out.
