Everything Sam Asghari Has Said About His Relationship With Britney Spears

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Popstar Britney Spears and personal trainer Sam Asghari met back in 2016 on the set of her music video for the song "Slumber Party." Ever since then, the two have been happily together and recently they even got engaged. While Britney has been in the spotlight for over two decades, fame is fairly new for the personal trainer.

Related: Britney Spears Says Her Engagement To Sam Asghari Was ‘Way Overdue’

Today, we're taking a look at everything Sam Asghari has said about his relationship with the famous singer. From why they almost never met to what they love doing together — keep on scrolling to find out!

7 Sam Admitted Britney's Happiness Is His Main Priority

Kicking the list off is the fact that Sam seems to prioritize Britney's happiness over anything. Here's what the personal trainer toldMen's Health:

"I just want her to be happy. If something makes her happy, I'll do it. I'm not going to argue. What's that saying? 'Happy wife, happy life.'"

6 And That The Two Love Working Out Together

According to Sam, the two love spending quality time together, and often that means getting a couple's workout in. Yes, the princess of pop is all about physical exercise, and here's what Sam revealed about their exercising regime:

"A lot of people don’t get that she’s a crazy, crazy athlete. We play tennis together. We play ping-pong together. She’s really good at ping-pong. It’s a real competition. And I’m competitive, but I try to take it easy. Not because she’s a woman. Not because she’s weak, because she’s not. But I grew up with three sisters, so I learned that taking competition too seriously can lead to hurt feelings. Family take it easy on each other. There's a lot of yoga that she likes to do. She's flexible, she has endurance, she does handstands on my legs. I'm not good at it, but I do it because she wants to do it."

5 Sam Openly Admitted What He Thinks About Britney's Conservatorship

Recently, the popstar publicly opened up about the struggles she has been going through with the conservatorship arrangement imposed in 2008. Her partner Sam also opened up about how it affects their relationship. Here's what the personal trainer wrote on Instagram:

"I have zero respect for someone trying to control our relationship and constantly throwing obstacles in our way. In my opinion, Jamie is a total d*ck."

4 Prior To Getting Engaged, Sam Admitted That Love Is More Than A Piece Of Paper

This month Britney Spears and Sam Asghari revealed that they got engaged — something both of them are very happy about. However, prior to the engagement, Sam revealed that while marriage is something the two plan — a piece of paper isn't that important to him. Here's what he said:

"We’ll see. Maybe. It’s ongoing. Maybe today, maybe tomorrow. But, you know, love isn’t just a piece of paper."

3 The Personal Trainer Joked About Their Prenup

While the two aren't married yet and no one knows what their prenup will include (and whether there will be one), Sam joked about the questions he got about it on Instagram. Here's what he said:

"Thank you to everyone who is concerned about the prenup. Of course, we're getting an ironclad prenup to protect my Jeep and shoe collection in case she dumps me one day."

2 And He Revealed He Would Always Support Her, No Matter What

It's no secret that Sam has been there for the popstar for five years — and he doesn't hide his support for the singer. Here's what the personal trainer revealed:

"I have always wanted nothing but the best for my better half, and will continue to support her following her dreams and creating the future she wants and deserves. I am thankful for all of the love and support she is receiving from her fans all over the world, and I am looking forward to a normal, amazing future together."

1 Lastly, Sam Said That He And Britney Almost Didn't Meet

The personal trainer revealed in an interview with Forbes that because he had already done some music videos he almost didn't do the one for Britney's "Slumber Party" where the two met. Here's what he said:

"I didn’t want to do more and be known as a music video actor, but a good friend of mine was working on a project and they referred me to the team that was picking out the leading role for ‘Slumber Party.’ My girlfriend now at the time, personally she picked my picture and she wanted me to be cast in the music video. My friend called me and said, ‘I need you to be on it. Trust me, you want to show up.’ I didn’t know who was shooting. It was a secret project. So I showed up because of my friend. I showed up and everything just kicked off from there"
