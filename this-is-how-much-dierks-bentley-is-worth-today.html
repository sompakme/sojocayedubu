This Is How Much Dierks Bentley Is Worth Today

Dierks Bentley has been a staple in country music since he first stepped onto the scene in 2003. In total, he has released an impressive ten studio albums over his two-decade career in the music industry. The musician has headlined 15 tours and co-headlined a tour with country music star Miranda Lambert. Bentley has also performed at Stagecoach, which is one of the biggest country music festival and occurs in Southern California.

Bentley’s first name is actually Frederick, but he uses his middle name as his stage name. He married his middle school sweetheart Cassidy Black in 2005, and together they have three children. Like many other country music stars, the Bentley family resides in Nashville, Tennessee. With his extremely impressive music career, Bentley has acquired a surprisingly high net worth. Here’s how much Dierks Bentley is worth today.

8 Who Is Dierks Bentley?

Dierks Bentley is an American country music singer. He first began releasing music in 2003, and over the past two decades he has released ten solo studio albums, an album with a band, and an EP. Bentley actually paid to record the EP Country & Cold Cans himself and released it in 2012, which was only a few months after he released his sixth studio album Home. Over the years, Bentley has headlined 15 tours and co-headlined a tour with country music singer Miranda Lambert.

Though Bentley is originally from Phoenix, Arizona, he now lives in the heart of country music: Nashville, Tennessee. Bentley has three children with his wife: Jordan, Knox, and Evalyn. Besides music, the singer also has a passion for flying. He has a private pilot license and owns a small plane.

7 What Did Frederick 'Dierks' Bentley Do Before He Was Famous?

Though he is known as Dierks Bentley publicly, the singer’s real first name is Frederick. He chose to use his middle name as his stage name. He was born in Phoenix, Arizona, but he moved around a lot for school. For college, he started at the University of Vermont. After a year, he transferred to Vanderbilt University in Nashville, which he graduated from in 1997.

Following college, Bentley began working at The Nashville Network, which is now known as Paramount Network. His job was to research old footage of country performances. He was banned from the Grand Ole Opry after he trespassed for what he claimed was research purposes. The ban was lifted when Bentley released his first studio album, Dierks Bentley in 2003.

6 Dierks Bentley Married His Middle School Sweetheart

Dierks Bentley and his wife Cassidy Black have a very sweet love story. They first met in 8th grade at Phoenix’s Ingleside Middle School. “I had a crush on her the whole time, but I wasn’t cool enough,” Bentley recalled to People in 2007. “I was way too immature and into drinking beer and guns and blowing things up.” Black agrees, claiming “he was just trouble” at the time. They attempted to date in high school, but things went awry when distance separated them. Bentley moved to Nashville, and Black moved to San Francisco.

“I used to write her all these crazy love letters that I’d FedExed out to her,” Bentley said of their long-distance relationship. “That’s why I married her! I was worried I’d get blackmailed with those letters!” Black still has every letter her husband wrote her. The two tied the knot in 2005 in a beautiful ceremony in Mexico. They now live in Nashville with their three children and dog George.

5 Dierks Bentley’s Early Days In Country Music

Dierks Bentley came in hot with his first album. Dierks Bentley was released in 2003 by Capitol Nashville, and the single “What Was I Thinking” reached number one on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. While other singers struggle at the beginning of their careers in the music industry, Bentley found success early and has continued to maintain that success throughout his career.

His second album Modern Day Drifter, which came out in 2005, was also very well received. He had two number one hits with that album titled “Come a Little Closer” and “Settle for a Showdown.” A third album called Long Trip Alone was released in 2006, and by 2008 Bentley was already releasing a Greatest Hits album. The singer allowed fans to choose songs for the album. Bentley has been nominated for 14 Grammy Awards, but he has never won one.

4 Dierks Bentley’s Band Hot Country Knights

Dierks Bentley took a break from releasing solo music in 2020 and performed with Hot Country Knights. The band was originally formed by Bentley in 2015. The band released its debut album in 2015 called The K Is Silent, and they have not released any music since. Bentley produced the album, and it was released by Capitol Records. He is also the lead singer of the Hot Country Knights, but he performs under the alter-ego Douglas “Doug” Douglasson.

The band itself and the album The K Is Silent is a parody of country music, specifically country music from the 1990s. The satirical tribute band is comprised of Bentley’s actual road band. The single “Pick Her Up” featured country music singer Travis Tritt. The band and the album received great reviews by critics, but they have not announced any plans to release a follow-up album.

3 Dierks Bentley Involves His Family In His Music

Dierks Bentley is first and foremost a family man. He has been in love with his wife Cassidy Black since they were in the 8th grade going to school in Phoenix, Arizona. Though he wants his three children to find their own paths in life, he is more than happy to include them in his world and the world of the music industry.

One of his daughters can be heard singing in one of Bentley’s songs. She provided vocals for the song “Thinking of You,” which appeared on Bentley’s 2012 album titled Home. Though his son has not exactly been involved in his music, he has also made an appearance. Bentley’s son speared in the music video for his 2019 single “Living.”

2 What Is Dierks Bentley’s Most Recent Album?

During that time, he has released ten studio albums, an album with the band Hot Country Knights, and an EP which he financed himself. Already that is an insane amount of music to have created, but Bentley has revealed that he often writes way more songs than what makes the cut of an album. Fans want Bentley to take a page out of Taylor Swift’s playbook and release these vault songs.

Bentley’s tenth studio album Gravel & Gold was released on February 24th, 2023. In preparation for the album, the country music singer released the single “Gold” on July 29th, 2022. He also released the single “High Note” in November 2023, which is more of a bluegrass song and features Billy Strings.

1 How Much Is Dierks Bentley Worth?

The money made from his music does not even include his 15 headlining tours or his appearances at Stagecoach, which is one of the biggest country music festivals and occurs in Southern California.
